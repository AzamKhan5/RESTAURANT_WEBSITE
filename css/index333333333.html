Welcome to The BAN Restaurant, a culinary haven founded by a trio of passionate individuals who share a common love for exceptional food and warm hospitality.

The story of The BAN Restaurant is one of friendship, innovation, and a shared love for great food. Born out of the collective dream of three engineer friends Bhangat Singh, Azam Khan and Nagesh Rathore in this culinary venture is a testament to their shared passion and expertise.

Our team of talented chefs is dedicated to crafting a menu that reflects a fusion of flavors, using only the finest and freshest ingredients. Whether you're savoring our signature dishes or indulging in our handcrafted desserts, each bite is a celebration of culinary excellence.

Beyond the delectable dishes, we take pride in providing a welcoming ambiance that invites you to relax and enjoy the moment. From the cozy interior to the attentive service, every aspect of The BAN Restaurant is designed to make your dining experience exceptional.
